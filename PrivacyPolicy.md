# Privacy Policy

Playback Cam and its developer take your privacy very seriously. Playback Cam uses no third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, tracking SDKs, login systems, or remote backend services. Playback Cam does not log personal information and has no interest in doing so.

Playback Cam works entirely on-device. The app uses the camera to show a live preview, record video clips, and provide delayed playback. Recorded clips are stored locally on your device while you review them. If you choose to save a clip, Playback Cam exports it locally and writes it to your Photo Library using Apple's system APIs.

When you allow Photo Library access, that permission is used only for the features you request:

- Add-only access is used to save clips to Photos
- Read/write access is used only if you choose to delete previously saved clips from Photos inside the app

Playback Cam does not upload, transmit, distribute, or sell your data. The app makes no HTTP requests and does not send your videos, device identifiers, or usage information to the developer or to third parties.

If you save media to the Apple Photos library, that content is then managed by Apple on your device and, depending on your personal iCloud settings, may also be handled by Apple's services under Apple's own terms and privacy policies.
